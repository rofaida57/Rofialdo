How to Play Pool Master

Game Objective

Be the first to pocket all your assigned balls (solids or stripes) and then legally pocket the 8-ball to win!

Gameplay

  • Players take turns shooting
  • Click and drag from the cue ball to aim
  • Release to shoot with selected power
  • First player to pocket a ball assigns ball types (solids or stripes)
  • Players must pocket their own balls first
  • After pocketing all your balls, target the 8-ball

Fouls

  • Pocketing the cue ball (scratch)
  • Pocketing opponent's ball
  • Hitting opponent's ball first
  • No ball hit or pocketed

After a foul, the opponent gets ball in hand.
